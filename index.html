Types of Notarization

What We Certify

Every notarization serves a distinct legal purpose. Here are the three most common types we handle daily.

Same Day

Acknowledgment

Confirms that a signer personally appeared, was properly identified, and signed the document of their own free will. Used for deeds, mortgages, and powers of attorney.

Learn More
Same Day

Jurat

Requires the signer to swear or affirm to the truthfulness of the document's contents in the notary's presence. Commonly used for affidavits and depositions.

Learn More
1–3 Business Days

Copy Certification

Certifies that a copy of an original document is true, exact, and complete. Accepted for diplomas, passports, contracts, and other critical personal records.
